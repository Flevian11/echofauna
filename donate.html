Why Your Support Matters

EchoFauna is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to preserving the memory of extinct species through cutting-edge technology. Unlike physical conservation, digital preservation ensures these species remain accessible to future generations regardless of physical constraints.

Research & Development
Your donations fund our AI research, 3D modeling, and scientific collaborations that bring extinct species back to digital life.
Education & Outreach
We create educational materials, virtual exhibits, and interactive experiences that teach about biodiversity loss and conservation.
Digital Preservation
Contributions help maintain our digital archives, server infrastructure, and ensure long-term accessibility of our reconstructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?
Donations directly fund our AI research, 3D modeling, educational content creation, and server infrastructure. We maintain transparency with quarterly reports available to all donors.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, EchoFauna is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.
Can I choose which species my donation supports?
While we can't allocate specific donations to individual species, you can suggest species for reconstruction. Our team prioritizes based on scientific value, available data, and educational impact.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and select cryptocurrencies. All payment methods are processed securely with encryption and fraud protection.
Can I set up recurring donations?
Yes! We offer monthly donation options that provide sustained support for our ongoing research and preservation efforts. Recurring donors receive exclusive updates.
